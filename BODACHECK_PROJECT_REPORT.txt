BodaCheck — Project Report
Digital Compliance & Safety ID System for Uganda's Boda Boda Riders
=====================================================================

1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
--------------------
BodaCheck is a lightweight digital compliance and safety ID system designed
for Uganda's 2 million+ boda boda motorcycle taxi riders. The system uses
QR codes linked to a central database to provide instant verification of a
rider's safety profile — their score, compliance status, and violation
history. Built with plain HTML, CSS, PHP, and MySQL on XAMPP, the system
requires no app installation for passengers and creates a permanent digital
paper trail for every enforcement action.

The system is designed for government ownership and operation, with
professional dashboards for KCCA, Uganda Police, and the Ministry of
Works & Transport. Revenue is generated through annual rider registration
fees (UGX 10,000-15,000/year), insurance referral partnerships, and B2G
licensing of the analytics platform.


2. THE PROBLEM
--------------
2.1 Scale of the Crisis
- 5,383 road deaths in Uganda in 2025
- 47% of all road fatalities are motorcyclists and their passengers
- 94% of Kampala boda riders have no driving licence
- 98% have no Public Service Vehicle permit
- 73% have no logbook
- 25,000 new motorcycles enter Kampala every month
- 32,308 riders were arrested for no helmet in 2024 — most paid their way out

2.2 Root Causes
- No barrier to entry: anyone buys or rents a bike and starts riding
- No fast verification: paper checks are slow, bribeable, and easily faked
- No passenger visibility: passengers cannot verify rider credentials
- Registration cannot keep up: KCCA capacity lags far behind motorcycle growth


3. THE SOLUTION
---------------
3.1 How BodaCheck Works
1. Rider registers with phone, bike plate, national ID, and SACCO membership
2. System generates a unique QR token that never changes
3. QR sticker is placed on the rider's helmet and bike
4. Passenger scans QR — sees rider profile in browser (no app needed)
5. Police scan at stops — instant verification with digital paper trail
6. Violations are logged — score updates live, status changes automatically

3.2 The Scoring System
- Every rider starts at 100 points
- No Helmet: -10 points
- No Licence: -20 points
- Reckless Driving: -25 points
- Overloading: -15 points
- Other: -10 points
- Score cannot go below 0
- Green status: 70-100 (Safe to Ride)
- Amber status: 40-69 (Caution)
- Red status: 0-39 (Not Safe)

3.3 Compliance Checklist
Each rider's profile shows their compliance status for:
- Helmet possession
- Driving licence
- PSV (Public Service Vehicle) permit
- Logbook
- Insurance coverage

3.4 MTN Mobile Money Integration
- Annual registration fee: UGX 10,000-15,000/year
- Riders pay via MTN MoMo from their dashboard
- Payment status tracked: paid, pending, unpaid
- Clean score riders qualify for lower insurance premiums


4. SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE
-----------------------
4.1 Technology Stack
- Frontend: HTML5, CSS3 (no frameworks)
- Backend: PHP 8.x (no Laravel, no Composer)
- Database: MySQL 8.x via phpMyAdmin
- Server: Apache (XAMPP)
- QR Generation: QR Server API with GD fallback
- Payments: MTN Mobile Money API (simulated)

4.3 Database Schema (6 Tables)
- saccos: SACCO organisations (name, district, contact)
- riders: Registered riders (profile, QR token, score, status, compliance, MoMo)
- users: Officers and admins (name, email, role, badge, active status)
- violations: Enforcement records (rider, officer, type, points, location, notes)
- scan_logs: QR scan audit trail (rider, scanner, type, IP, timestamp)
- payments: MTN MoMo transactions (rider, amount, phone, reference, status)


5. USER ROLES & ACCESS CONTROL
-------------------------------
5.1 General Public (Passenger)
- Scans QR code on rider's helmet
- Sees: name, photo, score, status badge, SACCO, insurance, compliance checklist
- No login required, no buttons, no editing
- 2-second UX: scan, see green or red, done

5.2 Rider
- Logs in with phone number + password
- Sees: own dashboard with score, QR code (downloadable), violation history
- Can edit: name, compliance items (helmet, licence, PSV, logbook, insurance)
- Can initiate MTN MoMo payment
- Cannot see other riders' data

5.3 Traffic Officer
- Logs in with email + password
- Can search any rider by QR token, phone, or bike plate
- Sees full rider profile with compliance checklist
- Can log violations (type, location, notes) — score updates automatically
- Every scan and violation is logged with badge number and timestamp

5.4 Administrator (Government)
- Logs in with email + password
- Full access to all data through government-styled dashboards
- Can create and deactivate officer accounts
- Can manually adjust rider scores with documented reason
- Can export all data as CSV (riders, violations, scans, payments)
- Access to analytics with national road safety context and revenue projections


6. SECURITY MEASURES
---------------------
- Passwords: Always hashed with password_hash() (bcrypt), verified with password_verify()
- SQL Injection: All queries use PDO prepared statements — no raw user input in SQL
- XSS: All output sanitized with htmlspecialchars() before rendering
- Session: Every protected page checks session and role at the top
- Access Control: Riders cannot see other riders; officers cannot access admin area
- RLS Equivalent: Role-based page protection with requireRole() function
- Audit Trail: Every QR scan and violation is logged with timestamp and user ID
- Data Minimisation: No GPS tracking, no real-time location data stored


7. GOVERNMENT ADMIN INTERFACE
------------------------------
The admin portal is designed for government operation with:

7.1 National Dashboard
- Key Performance Indicators: registered riders, violations, scans, revenue
- Rider status breakdown: green/amber/red with compliance bars
- Compliance rates: helmet, licence, PSV, insurance percentages
- Recent violations table with officer badge numbers
- Enforcement summary with scan activity

7.2 Analytics & Policy Intelligence
- Uganda road safety context (national baseline statistics)
- Compliance overview with visual progress bars
- Violation breakdown by type with percentage analysis
- Officer enforcement activity ranking
- SACCO distribution and performance comparison
- 30-day violation trend
- Revenue projections: current scale and full national scale

7.3 National Rider Registry
- Searchable table of all registered riders
- Score adjustment with documented reason
- Compliance status indicators (H=Helmet, L=Licence, P=PSV, B=Logbook, I=Insurance)
- Payment status tracking

7.4 Enforcement Log
- Full violation history with officer badge numbers
- Filters by violation type, date, and officer name
- Summary statistics for filtered results

7.5 Officer Personnel Management
- Create and manage officer accounts
- Activate/deactivate with violation count tracking
- Badge number assignment

7.6 Reports & Data Export
- CSV exports: Rider Registry, Enforcement Log, Scan Activity, Payment Records
- Government-standard file naming (BodaCheck_Rider_Registry_YYYY-MM-DD.csv)
- Data policy and privacy notice (Uganda Data Protection and Privacy Act 2019)


8. REVENUE MODEL
-----------------
8.1 Rider Subscriptions
- Annual registration fee: UGX 10,000-15,000/year
- Accessible to every rider — cheaper than a single bribe
- Paid via MTN Mobile Money

8.2 Insurance Integration
- Riders with green badges qualify for lower insurance premiums
- Referral fees from insurance partners
- Creates financial incentive for compliance

8.3 B2G Licensing
- Dashboard and analytics licensed to KCCA and Uganda Police
- Official registry tool for the government
- Model: private build, government adoption (like iRembo in Rwanda)

8.4 Revenue Projections
- Current scale: based on registered riders x fee x payment rate
- Full scale: 6,000 SACCOs x 500 riders x UGX 10,000 = UGX 30 billion/year potential

10. DEPLOYMENT INSTRUCTIONS
-----------------------------
1. Install XAMPP and start Apache and MySQL
2. Copy the bodacheck/ folder into htdocs/
3. Open phpMyAdmin (http://localhost/phpmyadmin)
4. Import db/bodacheck.sql to create the database and seed data
5. Open http://localhost/bodacheck/setup.php in your browser
6. This generates proper password hashes for all seed accounts
7. DELETE setup.php from the server after running it
8. Test the scan page with the URLs above
9. Login as admin to access the government dashboard
10. Login as officer to test search and violation logging


11. GO-TO-MARKET STRATEGY
--------------------------
- Start in one Kampala division (Makindye or Kawempe) with one SACCO partner
- Onboard 500 riders in month one at zero cost
- Demonstrate that green-badge riders earn 15-20% more per day from passenger preference
- Use that case study to onboard the next SACCO
- Uganda has over 6,000 registered boda SACCOs
- Once two or three adopt it, KCCA mandate follows naturally
- License the dashboard and analytics to KCCA and Uganda Police


12. COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGES
---------------------------
vs. Paper licensing drives: QR-linked digital records cannot be faked
vs. SafeBoda: Covers every independent rider, not just platform riders
vs. Police roadblocks: 3-second digital scan, no cash changes hands
vs. Awareness campaigns: Permanent, visible safety record that follows the rider


13. DATA PRIVACY COMMITMENT
-----------------------------
- Minimal data collection: phone, national ID, bike registration, crash log
- No GPS tracking, no real-time location
- Closer to a driver's licence record than a surveillance system
- Compliant with Uganda Data Protection and Privacy Act 2019
- Clear data policy published on the reports page
- Government records handling standards apply to all exports
